RadioMaster RP4TD Folding TPU Antenna Holders

Lightweight, flexible TPU holders designed to secure folding RP4TD antennas without interfering with your grip. Ideal for racers and freestyle pilots who need fast transport, durable protection, and a flight-ready radio setup.

These holders keep your antenna stable in flight while still allowing it to fold down neatly for storage and travel.

Note: this is a mount designed to attach to your drone setup.

Why this holder works

This holder is printed in flexible TPU to provide a secure friction fit, shock absorption, and smooth folding motion. It keeps the RP4TD antenna stable in flight while still allowing the antenna to fold down for easy transport.

  • Flexible TPU material for high durability and vibration dampening
  • Designed for folding RP4TD antenna layouts
  • Snap-fit design makes installation and removal fast and easy
  • Lightweight and compact, so it does not add bulk to your radio
  • Perfect for pilots who want a secure, lightweight antenna solution and a ready-to-fly radio
