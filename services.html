Printing Services

How it Works

  1. Send your model file by email or use the contact form.
  2. We review your design, recommend material, and confirm pricing.
  3. We print, clean, finish, and ship the part to your address.

What to Send

Use files like .STL, .OBJ, or .STEP. Include any notes about scale, color, and finish.

Need Help Finding a Model?

If you found a model online and just want it printed, we can handle the printing and shipping for you.

This is ideal when you don't have a 3D printer or access to a print shop.
